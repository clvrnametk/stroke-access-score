Data Update #1

LA County EMS Certification Correction

Published: June 8, 2026  ·  Source: Los Angeles County Emergency Medical Services Approved Stroke Center Directory, July 28, 2025

Replaced CMS quality rating proxy with official LA County EMS stroke certification data for 51 LA County hospitals. This is the first step toward full EMNet certification integration statewide.

51 Hospitals matched & updated
448 ZIP codes re-scored
218 Scores increased
34 Scores decreased

What changed and why

The original Stroke Access Score pipeline used CMS Hospital Compare overall quality ratings (1–5 stars) as a proxy for stroke center certification — assigning CSC to 5-star hospitals, TSC to 4-star hospitals, and PSC to all others. This was an explicit approximation, documented in the methodology, pending access to authoritative certification data.

This update replaces that proxy for LA County hospitals with the LA County EMS Approved Stroke Center Directory — the official list of facilities verified by the county's Emergency Medical Services agency. For the 51 hospitals matched, the tier assignment now reflects actual certification level rather than a quality rating estimate.

EMNet national stroke certification data remains pending and will trigger a full statewide update when it arrives.

Data Update #2

Scoring Model Revision & Dataset Cleanup

Published: June 8, 2026

Four corrections made in this update: TSC removed statewide as a proxy artifact; five non-certified LA County hospitals removed; eight hospitals missing from CMS added from the EMS directory; and the scoring model revised to a CSC-weighted structure with 15-minute drive time increments. Score decreases reflect more accurate data, not degraded care.

63 Hospitals de-certified (TSC removal)
8 Hospitals added (missing from CMS)
5 Hospitals removed (LA County non-certified)
CSC Scoring model revised (CSC-weighted)

Correction 1 — TSC removed statewide

TSC was assigned by CMS quality rating proxy only. LA County EMS and California EMS practice recognize only CSC and PSC. 63 hospitals nulled. 635 ZIPs re-scored. All changes flat or negative.

Correction 2 — Non-certified LA County hospitals removed

5 hospitals removed — not present on the LA County EMS Approved Stroke Center Directory (July 28, 2025):

  • Alhambra Hospital Medical Center
  • Glendale Memorial Hospital & Health Center
  • Martin Luther King Jr. Community Hospital
  • San Gabriel Valley Medical Center
  • VA Long Beach Healthcare System

Correction 3 — 8 missing hospitals added

Eight hospitals present in the LA County EMS directory were absent from the CMS dataset and added manually with drive-time collection.

CSC Added

  • Dignity Health Glendale Memorial
  • Dignity Health Northridge
  • Saint Francis Medical Center (Lynwood)
  • West Hills Hospital
  • PIH Health Whittier
  • Emanate Health Queen of the Valley (West Covina)

PSC Added

  • Dignity Health California Hospital Medical Center
  • Henry Mayo Newhall Hospital

Correction 4 — Scoring model revised

The previous model used a flat penalty structure. The revised model weights CSC access heavily with 15-minute drive time increments and a major penalty beyond 60 minutes. PSC provides modest mitigation only.

Score decreases in this update reflect more accurate data, not worse care. The revised model more closely follows clinical evidence on the impact of drive time to a thrombectomy-capable center. Lower scores represent a more honest picture of access.
