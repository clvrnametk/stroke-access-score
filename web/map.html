        Key Findings
        
The geography of stroke care inequality in California

        

          

            Income & Access
            —
            
Average score difference between top and bottom income quartile ZIP codes. Wealthier areas have consistently better proximity to certified stroke centers.

          

          

            Racial Disparity
            —
            
Average score gap between non-majority-minority and majority-minority ZIP codes. Communities of color face lower access to stroke care.

          

          

            Population at Risk
            —
            
Facing both poor access scores and elevated stroke risk factors including age and concentrated poverty.
